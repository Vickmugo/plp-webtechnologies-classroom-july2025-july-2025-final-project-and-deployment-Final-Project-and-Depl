Our Mission

At 254 Vinyls, our mission is to create a vibrant, global community of music enthusiasts. We believe music is a universal language that transcends borders and cultures, uniting people through shared melodies and rhythms.

Based in Kenya, we aim to connect African music lovers with the global vinyl scene, while also introducing international audiences to the richness and diversity of Kenyan and African music. Whether you're a collector, a casual listener, or someone discovering vinyl for the first time, we're here to bring love through music.

Our Vision

We envision a world where vinyl culture thrives as a bridge between generations and continents. From new releases to timeless classics, 254 Vinyls is committed to offering an unparalleled selection of titles, trade-in options, and weekly Black Friday-style sales. Our goal is to make every spin on your turntable a connection to the global heartbeat of music.

By embracing both Kenyan and international titles, we strive to empower local artists while celebrating the legends and innovators who have shaped the global soundscape.
